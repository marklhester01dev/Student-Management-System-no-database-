## Features

- **Add, view, edit, and delete** student records
- **Search** by Student ID, first name, last name, or course
- **Sort** by Student ID, name, course, or year level (ascending/descending)
- **Filter** by course, year level, or gender
- **Dashboard statistics** — total students, total courses, and breakdowns by year level and gender
- **Web Components** - buttons, modals, lists, cards
- **Persistent storage** — records are saved to `localStorage` and reloaded automatically on page refresh
- **Report Generation** - creation of reports downloadable through CSV/JSON file.
- **Form validation / Enrollment** — required fields, valid email/contact number, no duplicate Student IDs
- No backend, no build tools, no dependencies
